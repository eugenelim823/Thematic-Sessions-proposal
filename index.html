01
What is a Thematic Session?
The vehicle
Domain 领域
The form through which children engage —
drama, reading, philosophy.

The domain stays consistent
within a unit.
The destination
Theme 主题
The question the unit is really about —
democracy & power, emotion, speech.

Themes can rotate freely
across domains.
The shape
Arc 起承转合
Each 4-week unit follows a narrative arc —
introduction, immersion,
complication, resolution.

It begins and it ends.

Week
Focus
Activity

W1
One image
A wordless image. Each child "reads" it and tells what happened. No wrong answers.
Every reading is different
W2
One word changes everything
Same image. Each child receives a different prompt sentence. Read again. The story shifts completely.
A line defines a character drama surface
W3
Who is speaking?
A dialogue text — no names. Children decide who speaks, what their relationship is. Then swap and compare.
Roles are read in, not written drama + philosophy surface
W4
Return
Back to Week 1's image. Read it again. What do you see now?
